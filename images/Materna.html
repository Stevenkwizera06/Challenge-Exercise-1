Denver International Airport with automated baggage check-in

Denver International Airport has automated baggage check-in for its passengers as part of its comprehensive Great Hall redevelopment project. Materna IPS installed 86 bag drop machines and launched them in cooperation with United Airlines and Southwest Airlines - the largest self bag drop installation in the US.

More about the Self Bag Drop project

Topic: Denver Airport

Global self-service portal

Volkswagen Financial Services implemented a global self-service portal via the AWS Cloud. This solution simplifies IT support as well as ordering and provisioning processes. Materna brought extensive experience in the cloudification of applications to the project.

Topic: Software Development

Unified IT Service Management with BMC

IT service provider ITERGO has consolidated the ERGO Group's IT service management and now relies on BMC technologies throughout the group. Materna supported ITERGO during the consolidation and harmonization of its ITSM landscape and has established itself as a supplier of BMC technologies in the process.

Topic: ITERGO

UX concept puts users in the center of attention

Since its relaunch, the website of the German Chemical Industry Association (VCI) presents itself with an improved user experience, very accessible on mobile devices and visually very attractive. Materna developed a comprehensive user experience concept and took over the technical implementation.

Topic: German Chemical Industry Association

KfW Chatbot for student loans

Our chatbot experts have implemented a clever digital assistant for the KfW Bank website that answers questions about student loans. The chatbot convinces with a high recognition rate of user inputs, its intuitively usable user interface as well as the quick extensibility on the part of KfW.

Topic: KfW Chatbot

Mercedes-Benz Sales Germany Sales Portal

A new dealer portal improves communication between headquarters and the sales and service operations at Mercedes-Benz Vertrieb Deutschland. The automobile manufacturer has introduced a central content management system (CMS) for communication between headquarters and the associated dealerships and branches.

Topic: Mercedes-Benz

Daimler Financial Services: Agile software development with Scrum

The balance sheet planning tool COMET simplifies the planning process of the annual financial statements (balance sheets) for the worldwide subsidiaries of Daimler Financial Services AG. Materna implemented the web-based software application based on agile development methods.
